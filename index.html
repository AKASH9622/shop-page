Mango is fully ripe when the mangoes feel soft with a gentle squeeze and when you smell that fragrance around the stem end of the fruit. Don't go by the color, Red Mangoes doesn't mean they are ripe, red color around the stem of the mango doesn't mean it will taste better than other mangoes.

If the Mangoes are rock hard, It is not ripe yet, please store them at room temperature. Storing the Mangoes wrapped in newspaper or some brown paper fastens the process of ripening, this is one of the age old technique to ripe the Mango faster. Once the mangoes are ripe, i,e., softer to gentle squeeze, you can refrigerate for few days to prolong the shelf life after they are fully ripe.
